Hang on a sec…

Good evening. And welcome to everyone who’s joining us on the Internet.

Well, how did your week go? When we last talked I encouraged everyone to preface as many moments as you could with the thought from the Course:

“I do not know what anything, INCLUDING THIS, means.
And so I do not know how to respond to it.
And I will not use my own past learning as the light to guide me now.”1

So, when you turned on the news, or read the newspaper, or got out on the freeway, or cleaned up from the hurricane, or tried to deal with the lack of food or water or gasoline or money, did you say:

“I do not know what anything, INCLUDING THIS, means.”

Or did you, like Paul on many occasions, feel sure that you knew what it meant. Or maybe you felt sure that the feelings of frustration or anger … that you knew what they meant—that they were legitimate and that it was appropriate to dwell with them because you knew that they were justified.

Maybe what you were frustrated with or angry about shifted from moment to moment or circumstance to circumstance because it wasn’t really the specific issue that you were upset about. You were just upset, and anything was an appropriate object to vent your upsetness on.

It’s when you get bound up with what you are feeling—and what you are feeling are reactions—that you are unable to and unwilling to acknowledge that you …

“… do not know what anything, INCLUDING THIS, means.”

You experience commitment to your feeling. You feel commitment to the assurance that such-and-such is unjust and that you have a right to be upset, or angry, or jealous, or futile, or just plain old negative. And you savor it. You feel it. You turn it over in your mind and taste it, almost literally. And you get all the flavor out of it that you can because you’re so upset.

Emotions are addicting. Emotions are a trap. And I’m going to put it this way: we’ve talked many times about the Fall, about when you got a divorce from the Father—which is the definition or the meaning of the Fall—when you and a Brother or Sister said, “Let’s pretend, let’s imagine, let’s imaginatively give new definitions to Creation.” And in so many words, by that decision, you said, “See you later Father. We’re going to function on our own in ‘imagination land’ and we’re going to treat our imaginations as though they’re true. After all, we are omnipotent aren’t we? We are the full Expression of the Father.”

But as we’ve discussed, when that happened, two things came into play that had never existed before: fear and guilt. Fear came into play because when you are denying your Source, you’re in a state of self-denial. When you’re in a state of self-denial, you are in an unbalanced state of mind, an ungrounded state of mind. And because [chuckling] you are the holy Sons and Daughters of God, because you are divine, you are unable to create an ungrounded state of mind and be comfortable, because your holiness, your divine Sanity, your absolute groundedness confronts your imagination, and the deliberateness with which you embrace your imagination, and it’s uncomfortable. It is fright-ten-ing. And it’s supposed to be. And so, fear came into the picture.

Now, why? Why is there fear? Because you’re afraid you’ll be caught in a state of defying and denying the Father. And so, you’re trapped, because now you have to find a way to prove the legitimacy of your sense of yourself that isn’t your Self, but the imagined self that you and your Brother or Sister brought into play by behaving as though you weren’t the holy Son or Daughter of God, but something else—something independent.

It’s that simple.

Now, as I mentioned last time, and before: the Fall is something that is occurring now … now … now … now, in every moment in which you are choosing to exercise independence, practice independence—behave as though you are on your own with a capacity to think all by yourself.

Now, the way to break the guilt, the suffering, the fear is not to succeed in legitimizing yourself, but rather by abandoning the act of dissociation, the act of separation from your Source—from your Father. And you do that by acknowledging:

“I do not know what anything, INCLUDING THIS, means.
And so I do not know how to respond to it.
And I will not use my own past learning as the light to guide me now.”

You see the simplicity of this? It’s simple, but it’s hard to embrace because of the commitment you have to the self you think you are and what you’re going to make out of that self that you think you are.

Okay, it says in the Course:

I am not upset for the reason I think.2

I’m going to add something to it.

I am not upset for the reason I think. I am upset for the reason I think.

The first time it was said, the emphasis was on the word “reason”:

I am not upset for the REASON I think.

And the emphasis is on the word “think,” the second time:

I am upset for the reason I THINK.

Thinking gets you into trouble. Thinking is an act—an independent act—that is not arising out of your connectedness with your Self. What you are thinking on your own is so much static, so much noise—meaninglessness.

Now, you are always upset because of what you are thinking. You are always upset because you are engaged in thinking, no matter what the subject is. The holy instant, the two-step, is the process of abandoning the thought process, because that is what undoes the Fall. And that is the issue of what we have been discussing and what we are continuing to discuss tonight.

Now it is important to see that reaction, emotion is a trap because, as happened in the Fall, when fear occurs, you become distracted from your omnipotence, you become distracted from your holiness, you become distracted from your Source. And the fear present engages you in attempting to overcome the fear instead of abandoning what caused it. And in the process of overcoming the fear, you attempt to legitimize the “self” you now find yourself experiencing as “you”—which is a fake, which is artificial, which is imaginary.

And so, you end up in a perpetual state of self-denial, that you are perceiving as self-affirmation, because you are engaged in trying to affirm the illusory self that has grasped your attention and trapped you.

When you are in that state, it does not seem sensible in the slightest to say:

“I do not know what anything, INCLUDING THIS, means.”

It does not make sense to say:

“I do not know how to respond to it.”

And it doesn’t make sense to say:

“I will not use my own past learning as the light to guide me now.”

You’re caught in the trap. And the trap says, “You must use your past learning as the light to guide you now.” “It would be insane for you to set down your fear.” “It would be insane for you to become still, to seek peace, because if you do that, everything you are afraid of will come upon you and destroy you. And then you’ll know the truth—the awful truth—and you will be sorry.”

Now, that’s the way the illusion works, that’s the way existence after the Fall functions. And that’s what keeps you in a fallen state, even though you, the holy Son or Daughter of God that you Are, has not been altered in the slightest. You are still the holy Son or Daughter of God. You are still the effect of God. You are still the manifestation of what God is Being right there where you are. That’s unalterable. And that natural eternal experience is present right now, right where you are, while you are preoccupied with your fearful sense of being a separate self, which is attempting to bring forward the courage and the strength and the power to be able to legitimize yourself in this altered state—all of which seems good and respectable and worth every ounce of energy you can apply to it.

“What better thing could there be to do than to improve yourself? That is the purpose of life … to improve yourself.” But it isn’t, it isn’t! The need is to choose for your peace in spite of it, in spite of everything—to choose for your peace because, in your peace and in the absence of the struggle to improve yourself, the wholeness of you has room to register with you and invite you Home by the very nature of the experience. And that’s the whole point.

And so, in one way or another it must become clear to you that it is sensible, reasonable and natural to acknowledge that you do not know at this moment, in this frame of mind that you’re in…

“… what anything, INCLUDING THIS, means.

And so I do not know how to respond to it.

And I will not use my own past learning as the light to guide me now”…

… because I don’t want to be sucked back into and held hostage to a false sense of everything that is going on, including an utterly false sense of who and what I am.

Somewhere along the line this idea has to become familiar to you—whether it’s understandable or not, whether you are experiencing it or not, whether it seems possible or not—because if this idea doesn’t begin to percolate and synthesize in your awareness, if it doesn’t, you won’t give your attention to anything beyond the state of self-protection and self-preservation that seems normal.

Now, continuing from where we left off last time, which is the 3rd full paragraph on page 344 of the Sparkly book …

How can you, so firmly bound to guilt and committed so to remain, establish for yourself your guiltlessness? That is impossible. But be sure that you are willing to acknowledge that it IS impossible.

That’s why we must talk about this, so that the idea of what is true is present to give you justification to make the “idiotic” statement that it is impossible for you to establish your guiltlessness. [Whispering] Why … why? So that you’ll stop trying.

All of the effort to become something, all of the effort to improve yourself is to overcome guilt. You’re running away from an illusion—something that isn’t Real. You are not guilty. You are not the mortal you think you are, or that you seem to be. You are, and can’t ever stop being, the holy Son or Daughter of God. That means right now.

So, somewhere along the line here, the idea needs to be present in your mind clearly enough for you to even just imaginatively say, “I can’t establish my guiltlessness. And so, I’m going to stop trying.”

“I will try it out. I will experiment. I will dare to abandon the fear-motivated intense actions that I have felt I must engage in to improve myself and to become something.”

It is only because you think that you can run some little part, or deal with certain aspects of your lives alone, that the guidance of the Holy Spirit is limited.

But it’s the little ones that get you. It’s the little ones that sidetrack you. Someone says something to you with a certain tone of voice, which might have been occasioned by gas in their intestines—pain—and you hear it and you think that it is making a statement about you, a derogatory statement about you, and you’re quite sure of it.

Well, hey, that isn’t a universal truth or a challenge of a universal issue. It’s one of the “little” things. [Chuckling] It’s really inconsequential. But the reaction from that little misperception is all it takes to engage you in emotion, fear and guilt and the need for self-protection! The need for self-justification! The need to stand up for yourself! The need to prove yourself! The need to make sure that your validity and worth and respectability is recognized! And you’re willing to do it vehemently because it is a good cause!

No it’s not!

It’s a trap! A little inconsequential thing like a mosquito buzzing around your ear that has nothing to do with the family gathering at a picnic on a sunny afternoon in a park, where everyone is having fun. Oh, but no, that mosquito buzzing in your ear gets your attention and gets you frustrated and even makes you say, “Why do we always have to have a picnic? Why can’t we have a family get-together someplace where there aren’t mosquitos?” … grouse … grouse … grouse … fuss. Really!

It is only because you think that you can run some little part, or deal with certain aspects of your lives alone, that the guidance of the Holy Spirit is limited.

Well, of course. You withdraw your attention from the holiness of you, from the divine nature and character of you which is Love, which sees peace and is at peace. And so, all that comes through the avenue of peace, is lost to you … even though it’s there.

So all of it is just a misperception of the ultimate which is going on at this very moment.

What would the Holy Spirit have you give? The willingness to see your Brother and your world with His Vision so that you might be Awake, so that you might experience your Wholeness, so that you might experience your peace.

You have NO problems which He cannot solve by offering you a miracle.

A miracle—a sudden shift of perception, right here, right now … not pie-in-the-sky after you die.

Miracles are for YOU.

Miracles are for you.

Four words … four of the biggest words there are.

Miracles are for YOU.

They are for no one else but you. Miracles exist for you. You are the reason miracles exist! Does that say it clearly enough?

You are the reason miracles exist. Why? Because you’re the holy Son or Daughter of God who has forgotten who He or She is and the miracle is the means by which you are returned to your right Mind. And that’s the only thing they are for. That is their only purpose.

Miracles are for YOU.

And once you accept the miracle, once you let it in, once you desire it, you Wake up, and the miracle is no longer needed because there’s no “you” calling for it. There’s no “you” needing to Awaken.

So, understand this: Miracles aren’t some marvelous events that go on in the universe for “God only knows what reason.” Miracles—specifically and only—are for you. They exist because God, your divinity, insists upon your not being deluded … because there is that much Love.

Miracles are Love waiting for you to let It in. And that’s the only reason miracles exist. You are the only reason miracles exist.

Miracles are not favors doled out, “If you’re a good boy or girl.” Miracles are your very own divinity registering with you, when you stop trying to act independently and become still enough for them—for Who and What you divinely Are—to register with you and snap you back into your right Mind … bring you Home.

Marvelous four words:

Miracles are for YOU. And every fear or pain or trial you have HAS BEEN undone.

That means it never was a fact because you never were anything less than the holy Son or Daughter of God for whom the miracle exists.

But I’m telling you that the divine one, the holy one that you Are, is not only in the foreground, it is the ground of Being of you right now. And, literally, every misperception is a fantasy. Misperceptions are not hard to get rid of. They’re not like the Rock of Gibraltar, a humongous mass of heaviness.

There are no dark lessons. There is no darkness. There is no self-centered, self-establishing self in you.

Me and the Holy Spirit, that disappears. What’s left is “I Am.” And the I Am-ness of it is God Expressing Himself because the I of You is letting It in, and through, and confirming that It’s All God and not a separate entity called you being God’s spokesman, or God’s actor, or God’s delivery boy.

Now, when one engages in the two-step, when one insists on not using his own past learning as the light to guide him now, something unexpected happens just as in the beginning. But it’s the reverse: division disappears … division disappears. Conflict vanishes. Peace is restored surprisingly. Surprising, because you wouldn’t expect that to happen from your becoming still, from your not engaging is some grand act. But there it is: division disappears.

That’s the surprise that becomes clear to you, that’s the experience that unexpectedly infills you.

It came as a wholeness, not as a little part added to another little part, linearly; the whole thing came in a sudden flash because understanding is whole. And it comes in the middle of peace.

Self-righteousness isn’t that great.

You think you know Him not only because, alone, it is impossible to know Him. Yet …

… listen to this …

… see the mighty works that He will do through you, and you must be convinced you did them through Him.

[Raj says a few words here, but I the static is too strong to be able to make out the words well enough to transcribe.]

It is impossible to deny the Source of effects so powerful they COULD not be of you.

You see? We talked about this before. How insulting is that? How insulting for big things to happen which weren’t at your hand, but [chuckling] you seem to be the delivery boy for.

Again, that’s still holding on to the delusion about you being not just independent in mind but independent in manifestation. You are the Mind that is God Presenting Himself. But you won’t experience what God is Presenting until you stop trying to take credit for it yourself.

Leave room for Him, and you will find yourself so filled with power that NOTHING will prevail against your peace.

[Chuckling] You notice it didn’t say, “Nothing will prevail against you.” Having a you on your mind to prevail or be prevailed against is part of the illusion.

Leave room for Him, and you will find yourself so filled with power that NOTHING will prevail against your peace.

And if nothing is prevailing against your peace, understanding will be your experience of Being, and it will be an infinite understanding.

And this will be the test by which you recognize that you have understood.

This is simply the Truth. I love you all and I look forward to being with you next time.
